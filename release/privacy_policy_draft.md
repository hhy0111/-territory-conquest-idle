## 1. Overview

Territory Conquest Idle is an offline-first single-player mobile game. The game stores progression locally on the device and may display advertisements through third-party ad services on supported Android builds.

## 2. Information We Process

### Local save data

The game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

- profile progression
- meta upgrades
- active run state
- run results

This local save data is used to restore progress and support gameplay continuity.

### Advertising and consent information

On Android builds with advertising enabled, the game may use Google AdMob and the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP). These services may process information such as:

- advertising identifiers
- device and app information
- consent status
- ad interaction status

This information is used to request, display, and measure ads and to manage consent requirements.

### Diagnostic and QA information

The current project records local ad event traces for QA and verification flows. These records are intended for debugging and validation and are not currently wired to a production analytics backend in this repository state.

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- save and restore game progress
- resume interrupted runs
- determine ad eligibility and consent state
- display rewarded, interstitial, and app-open ads where enabled
- verify ad success and failure paths during QA

## 4. Third-Party Services

Android builds may use:

- Google AdMob
- Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform requirements.

## 5. Data Sharing

The game does not currently require account creation or player login. Local gameplay progression is stored on-device. Third-party advertising services may process data necessary to serve ads, manage consent, and detect abuse or invalid traffic.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Territory Conquest Idle is not directed to children under the age threshold defined by applicable platform policy unless explicitly reclassified before release. If release targeting changes, this policy and the app’s ad configuration must be updated accordingly.

## 7. Data Retention

Local save data remains on the device until the app data is cleared or the app is uninstalled, subject to platform behavior.

## 8. Your Choices

Depending on your region and platform behavior, you may be presented with a consent flow for advertising through UMP. You can also stop using the app or clear app data through your device settings.
